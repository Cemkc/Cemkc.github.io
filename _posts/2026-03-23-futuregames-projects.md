## 📌 Overview: Rapid Agile Production
During my curriculum at Futuregames, I participated in intensive 3-weeks game production cycles. Collaborating in large, 12-person cross-functional teams containing programmers, designers, artists, and producers, we developed polished Unity titles from scratch under strict deadlines. 

Both projects were pitched to and judged by industry veterans from prestige studios including **CD Projekt Red** and **11 Bit Studios**. Below are the two award-winning titles I helped develop.

***

## 🍌 Nana: Into the Banana (1st Place Winner)

### 🛠️ Tech Stack & Role
* **Role:** Programming Lead & Combat System Programmer
* **Engine:** Unity 3D / C#
* **Production Cycle:** 3 weeks

### 🚀 Technical & Production Highlights
* **Combat System Architecture:** Engineered highly responsive, scalable core combat mechanics from the ground up using C#, designed to be easily tunable by game designers.
* **Programming Leadership:** Coordinated technical execution across the multi-disciplinary team, enforcing clean architecture and strict version control practices to prevent merge conflicts during the rapid development cycle.
* **Links:** [Play on Itch.io](https://futuregames.itch.io/nanaintothebanana)

## ❄️ The Polar Protocol (2nd Place Winner)

### 🛠️ Tech Stack & Role
* **Role:** AI Programmer
* **Engine:** Unity 3D / C#
* **Production Cycle:** 3 weeks

### 🚀 Technical Highlights
* **AI Behavior & Agent Logic:** Spearheaded the artificial intelligence systems, utilizing and extending Unity's Behavior Tree packages to engineer complex, responsive agent logic.
* **Dynamic State Management:** Designed modular AI states that allowed enemies to react dynamically to player inputs and environmental changes, adding depth to the gameplay loop without bottlenecking the CPU.
